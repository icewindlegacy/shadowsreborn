AUTODAMAGE

****************************

Syntax: autodamage

Toggles the automatic display of damage dealt in combat. When enabled,
you will see exact damage numbers during fights. When disabled, you'll

COMM STONES

****************************

Syntax: <frequency> <message>
        <frequency> emote <action>
        freq <channel> <frequency>

Commstones are magical devices that allow long-distance communication on
specific frequencies. You can have up to 4 commstone channels active at
once.

TRANSMIT: Simply type the frequency number followed by your message:

TUNE: Configure your commstone channels:
  tune 1 42    - Set channel 1 to frequency 42

allowing for private group communication. Drunk speech and common language
effects apply to commstone communications!

0 QUESTING QUEST~

QUESTING

****************************

Syntax: quest info
        quest list
        quest buy <item>
        quest request
        quest complete
        quest points
        quest transfer <amount> <player>
        quest quit

The questing system allows you to undertake special missions for rewards.
Quest masters can be found in major cities and will offer tasks suitable
to your level.

Certain characters in the game will allow you to go on quests to recover an
item or kill another mob. To go on a quest, you must be in the same room with
a questmaster. When you successfully complete a quest, you will receive a
certain number of quest points. These points may be saved up and used to buy
incredible items from the questmaster.

QUEST INFO - If you are on a quest, this will remind you who or what you are
after.

QUEST LIST - Displays the list of fabulous items for sale by the questmaster.
These items are not buyable via gold, they must be purchased with quest points.

QUEST BUY <item> - Purchases an item from the questmaster and deducts the cost
of the item from your quest points.

QUEST REQUEST - Petitions the questmaster to send you on a quest. If you
accept the quest, you will be given a realtime limit (in minutes) in which you
must complete the quest. If you are on a quest to kill a creature, when you
come across that creature you will see a [TARGET] notice before it. This is
the creature you are to kill.

QUEST COMPLETE - Once you have completed your quest and recovered the item or
killed the mob or whatever, you must return to the questmaster who sent you on
the quest and use this command. If you have successfully completed your quest,
he will reward you with gold and quest points. Once you complete a quest you
cannot request another for 10 minutes.

QUEST POINTS - Gives your current number of quest points. This command may be
used anywhere.

QUEST TRANSFER - Transfers a number of quest points from you to another player.

QUEST TIME - Gives the amount of time remaining on your current quest, or the
amount of time until you can request another quest. This command may be used
anywhere.

QUEST QUIT - Abandon your current quest (with penalty)

Quest rewards include:
- Quest points (QP)
- Gold and experience

Quest points can be spent at quest shops for unique items not available
elsewhere in the game.

KEYRING

        keyring add <key>
        keyring remove <key>

Keyrings are special containers that store keys without taking up
inventory space. Once a key is added to your keyring, it will

52 PLOAD PUNLOAD~

PLOAD / PUNLOAD

****************************

Syntax: pload <player>
        punload <player>

(Immortal Command - Level 52+)

PLOAD - Load an offline player's character data into memory for editing
or inspection. This allows immortals to modify offline characters or
investigate issues without requiring the player to be online.

PUNLOAD - Unload a player that was loaded with PLOAD, saving any changes
made while they were loaded.

WARNING: Be extremely careful when using these commands! Modifying offline
characters can cause serious problems if not done correctly. Always:
- Note what changes you make
- Save the character after modifications
- Unload the character when finished
- Never leave characters loaded indefinitely

These commands are essential tools for customer service and administration.
